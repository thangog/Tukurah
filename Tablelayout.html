All anacondas live in South America east of the Andes. Green anacondas are found in the Brazilian Amazon basin, the Orinoco basin, Colombia, Venezuela, Ecuador,Peru, Bolivia, Trinidad and the Guianas, Jacksonville Zoo states.

The green anaconda is the largest snake in the world, when both weight and length are considered.It can reach a length of 30 feet (9 meters) and weigh up to 550 (227 kilograms). To picture how big that is if about five ten-year-olds lie down head to foot, they'd be about the length of this huge snake.
